About me....

My affinity for art has been a constant companion throughout my life.
Since 2016, I've immersed myself in the study, design, and creation of tattoos. The conception of my ideal creative space took shape during my time abroad. Exploring various studio environments and experiencing tattoos within similar settings fueled my desire to establish a space of my own. I cater to tattoo collectors seeking a distinctive and individualized experience. Every piece crafted within our brick walls reflects a commitment to craftsmanship and dedication.
